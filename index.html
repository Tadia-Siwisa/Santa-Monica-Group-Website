Remgro Limited
Diversified South African investment holding company with interests in healthcare, financial services, consumer products, and industrial sectors. Listed on the JSE.

Investment Thesis

Remgro is one of South Africa's premier investment holding companies, with a portfolio spanning healthcare (Mediclinic), financial services (FirstRand, RMB Holdings), consumer products, and industrial assets. Founded by Dr Anton Rupert in the 1940s, it delivers consistent dividend growth and capital appreciation through long-term strategic partnerships.

Masimong is a diversified majority black-owned and controlled investment company led by Mike Teke.
